How I Build Systems

I focus on clarity before code: understanding data flow, defining contracts, and modeling failure scenarios early.

I prioritize readable APIs, stable schemas, and defensive programming. Logging, monitoring, and clear deployment steps are treated as part of the system, not afterthoughts.

My goal is always the same: systems that behave predictably under load, are easy to reason about, and can be maintained by any competent engineer.
